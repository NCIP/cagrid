Operations

The developer can add, remove, or modify operations on the service. To add an operation click on the Services tab as descibed in the ealier section, and then select the service in the tree that you wish to add the operation to. Once you have selected the service you can then select the Add Method button from the panel on the right. For modifying or removing an operation just select the operation from the Services tree on the left and select the Modify or Remove method button from the panel on the right.
