On behalf of the caGrid team, I am pleased to announce the release of caGrid Portal version 2.2. 

Enhancements in this release include:
   * Enabled aggressive information caching and tuned the database to improve overall performance.
   * Upgraded Liferay Portal to 5.0.1 to correct several bugs and reduce page-load times. 
   * Added a "Sign in" and "Register" link to all pages.
   * Implemented recommendations for Section 508 compliance.

We invite you to visit the updated caGrid Portal: 
  http://cagrid-portal.nci.nih.gov

A complete list of new features, remediated defects, and known issues can be found in the release notes: 
  https://gforge.nci.nih.gov/plugins/scmcvs/cvsweb.php/cagrid-1-0/Applications/cagrid-portal/RELEASE_NOTES.txt?rev=1.2.14.2;content-type=text%2Fplain;cvsroot=cagrid-1-0
